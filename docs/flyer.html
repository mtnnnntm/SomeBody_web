Do you still remember that you have a body when working at a computer? SomeBody is a set of tools meant to remind you that you are not a machine.
__ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _
👁️
Posture Police
Watches your posture through your webcam and nudges you when it gets bad.
💨
Breathing Guide
Gently guides your breathing with customizable visuals and sounds.
✋
Hand Trainer
Imagine your computer screen as a window that gets dirtier the longer you use it. Wipe it clean with your hands.
