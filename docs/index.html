Do you still remember that you have a body when working at a computer? SomeBody is a set of tools meant to remind you that you are not a machine. Experience it live here, or download the desktop apps.

__ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __ _ __
1. Posture Police
👁️ PosturePolice

WHAT IT DOES

Uses your webcam to track body keypoints and monitor your posture in real time.

SCORING

It scores four dimensions: sitting duration, eye distance to the screen, neck tilt, and back alignment.
neck angle too bent? ─> neck score +1
back slouching? ─> back score +1
eye too close? –> eyes score +1
sitting for too long? –> sitting score +1

PENALTIES

When the border turns orange, it’s time to improve your posture! Otherwise different strategies will be used to make you aware.

2. Breathing Guide
💨 BreathingGuide

WHAT IT DOES

Displays animated visuals that expand and contract in sync with a breathing rhythm, helping you restore deeper breathing without having to pause what you’re working on.

CUSTOMIZE

Pick a visual mode, a colour, a sound, and a timer that work best for you, and breath along:
inhale → hold → exhale → rest

3. Hand Trainer
✋ HandTrainer

WHAT IT DOES

Imagine your computer screen as a window that gets dirtier the longer you use it. Wipe away the mouse traces and text using different sides of your palms.

BONUS FEATURE

When your computer is not in use for 20 minutes, it automatically enters finger-drawing mode, where you can do the opposite: pinch your fingers to draw, then erase with the cursor.

GESTURE OVERVIEW

✋ = erase dirt/keydrop
🤏 = draw
✌️ = screenshot overlay
✌️✌️ = screenshot full desktop
